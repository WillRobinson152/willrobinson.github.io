Will Robinson is a detail-oriented problem solver who strives to inform decision makers with clear, accurate and timely data.

His data skillset includes data acquisition (via API or scraping), cleaning, visualization (in Tableau, Power BI, Flourish, Datawrapper and Everviz), analysis and storytelling. His coding skillset is deepest in Python, SQL, JavaScript and Google Apps Script.

Robinson holds two degrees from the University of Georgia, as well as a professional certificate in data science from Springboard and a full stack developer professional coding certificate from MIT xPro.

Will Robinson is an experienced editor and journalist who specializes in financial reporting and data analysis.

As a journalist, he has covered a variety of subjects, including commodity markets, real estate, logistics, technology, finance, economic development, energy and education. He has published a number of investigative series, and his work has won recognition from national and state news associations.
