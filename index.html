Will Robinson

Will Robinson is an experienced editor and journalist who specializes in financial reporting and data analysis.

As a journalist, he has covered a variety of subjects, including commodity markets, real estate, logistics, technology, finance, economic development, energy and education. He has published a number of investigative series, and his work has won recognition from national and state news associations.

Robinson has accumulated data and digital skills through various certifications that enable him to bring context and clarity to his articles. He has advanced skills in Python, Excel, Tableau and Power BI and is proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React and SQL.

As an editor at OPIS, a Dow Jones company, Robinson has deployed these skills to create a spreadsheet used to assess prices, craft data visualizations, automate data collection and content generation, build a user interface for an internal API and make a tool used to convert screenshots of market data into a structured spreadsheet.

Robinson is a magna cum laude graduate from the University of Georgia Honors College with degrees in journalism and international affairs.
