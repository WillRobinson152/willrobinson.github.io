Data Scientist

From September 2020 to April 2021, Robinson completed the Springboard Data Science Career Track, a six-month intensive course that equipped him with the technical skills to complete the six steps of the data science method: problem identification, data wrangling, exploratory data analysis, pre-processing and training data development, modeling, and documentation. The course covered statistics, machine learning, Python, SQL, Tableau and more. The course structure is available here.

Prior to the Springboard program, Robinson earned an IBM Data Science Professional Certificate through Coursera and a Business Analytics Nanodegree through Udacity.
