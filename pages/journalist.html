Journalist

Robinson has published hundreds of market commentaries and news alerts for OPIS, a Dow Jones company, where he is currently a senior editor of natural gas liquids. Previously, Robinson reported for the Jacksonville Business Journal, originally as the logistics, tech and finance reporter and later as the real estate and economic development reporter. Prior to that, he interned at the Atlanta Journal-Constitution, where he covered education; at Fast Copy News Service, a freelance service wherein he was published by the AJC, Engineering Georgia and Gulfshore Business Magazine; and at the Atlanta Business Chronicle, where he wrote profiles and trend stories.
